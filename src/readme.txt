Version 2.0.4.2

To be run in eclipse as a project.

Three games are included:

Chinese Checkers - Prepare for the thrill ride of a lifetime as you slowly move pieces towards the other side of the board

 - The hint button shows all available moves for your player, denoted as a green circle as a tile to move into
 - Up to six local players
 - Network play for two players

Mancala - Are you up to the challenge of collecting more pebbles than your opponent? Find out in this radical game

 - Network play for two players
 
Senet - Throw sticks! Move pieces! This adrenaline fueled game has it all

 - Hints to show where you can place a piece
 - Network play for two players
 
General Features

 - Different difficulties of AI
 - Settings for each game, stored in a properties file
